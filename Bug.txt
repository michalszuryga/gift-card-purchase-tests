Title: 'Required' message won't disappear when 'Other' amount and then 50/100/150 USD is selected

Description:
Upon selecting the 'Other' option for the amount field and then choosing 50, 100, or 150 dollars from the available options, the 'Required' message persists on the screen.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to the Gift Card Value section on the website.
2. Click the 'Other' checkbox.
3. Select either 50, 100, or 150 euros from the available options.

Actual Result:
The 'Required' message remains visible on the screen, even after selecting a valid amount of 50/100/150 dollars.

Expected Result:
Upon selecting a valid amount from the dropdown menu, the 'Required' message should disappear.

Environment:
Operating System: Windows 10
Browser: Google Chrome version 98.0.4758.102
Device: Desktop
Product environment: demo website
